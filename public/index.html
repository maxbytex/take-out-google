📷 Google Photos Downloader

Uses Google Photos Picker API — select photos & download organized by date

Authentication

Sign in with your Google account to start.

Download your photos

The Picker API lets you select which photos to download. Google opens a secure picker UI where you choose your photos. To select all photos, use Ctrl+A / Cmd+A inside the picker.

1

Open Google Photos Picker

A Google-hosted picker will open in a new tab. Select the photos you want to download, then come back here.

2

Confirm selection

Once you've selected your photos in the picker and clicked "Done", click the button below to check.

3

Download

Photos will be saved organized by YYYY/MM/DD/photos|videos/ (+ camera model if available).

📦 Process Google Takeout export

Download your photos via Google Takeout (takeout.google.com), extract the ZIP, then enter the path to the extracted folder below. The app will read GPS from the JSON sidecar files and organize everything into YYYY/MM/DD/Country/City/photos|videos/.

Output folder: downloads/ on the server (same structure as Picker downloads).
